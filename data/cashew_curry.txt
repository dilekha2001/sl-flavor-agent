Sri Lankan Cashew Curry (Kaju Curry)

Description: A rich, slightly sweet curry made from cashew nuts simmered in coconut milk. A festive dish often served during special occasions and holidays.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 2 cups raw cashew nuts, soaked overnight
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 250 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp sugar
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Drain soaked cashews and set aside.
2. Heat oil, add cinnamon, curry leaves, and onion. Sauté until soft.
3. Add curry powder, chili powder, and turmeric, cook briefly.
4. Add cashews and enough water to just cover them, simmer for 15-20 minutes until tender.
5. Stir in coconut milk and sugar, simmer 5 more minutes until gravy thickens. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to a pinch, increase sugar slightly for sweetness.
- Medium (3): 1/2 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): 1.5-2 tsp chili powder plus a slit green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 380 kcal | Protein: 9g | Fat: 26g | Carbohydrates: 26g | Sodium: 220mg
