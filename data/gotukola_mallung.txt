Sri Lankan Gotukola Mallung

Description: A finely shredded raw green leaf salad mixed with grated coconut, lime, and a light tempering of spices. A nutritious, refreshing side dish often eaten to balance richer curries.

Ingredients (serves 4 as a side):
- 2 cups gotukola leaves (pennywort), finely shredded (substitute with finely shredded spinach if unavailable)
- 1/2 cup grated coconut
- 1 small onion or 2 shallots, finely chopped
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 green chili, finely chopped
- 1 tsp lime juice
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Combine shredded gotukola leaves with grated coconut, onion, turmeric, and green chili in a bowl.
2. Mix in lime juice and salt.
3. Toss everything together thoroughly by hand until well combined and slightly softened.
4. Serve fresh at room temperature.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): omit the green chili.
- Medium (3): 1 chili as listed.
- Hot (4-5): 2 chilies, finely chopped.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 90 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 6g | Carbohydrates: 7g | Sodium: 120mg
