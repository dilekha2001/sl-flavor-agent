Sri Lankan Curd and Treacle (Kithul Mee Peni saha Kiri Peni)

Description: A simple, beloved dessert of thick buffalo curd drizzled with kithul treacle (palm syrup). Often the finishing touch to a meal, especially at celebrations.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 4 cups buffalo curd (or thick set yogurt as a substitute)
- 4-6 tbsp kithul treacle (palm syrup) or substitute with honey/maple syrup

Steps:
1. Spoon curd into individual serving bowls.
2. Drizzle generously with kithul treacle just before serving.
3. Serve chilled.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- This dessert contains no chili or heat; it is a sweet dish and spice-level controls do not apply.

Approximate nutrition (per serving):
Calories: 210 kcal | Protein: 7g | Fat: 8g | Carbohydrates: 28g | Sodium: 70mg
