Sri Lankan Egg Curry

Description: Hard-boiled eggs simmered in a spiced coconut milk gravy. A quick, popular curry often made when fresh meat or fish isn't available.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 6 eggs, hard-boiled and peeled
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 inch ginger, chopped
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 250 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Lightly fry the boiled eggs in a little oil until the surface blisters slightly, then set aside.
2. In the same pan, sauté onion, garlic, ginger, and curry leaves until soft.
3. Add curry powder, chili powder, and turmeric, cook briefly until fragrant.
4. Pour in coconut milk, bring to a simmer.
5. Add the fried eggs, simmer for 8-10 minutes until the gravy thickens slightly. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to a pinch.
- Medium (3): 1/2 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): increase chili powder to 1.5 tsp and add a slit green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 280 kcal | Protein: 14g | Fat: 20g | Carbohydrates: 8g | Sodium: 340mg
