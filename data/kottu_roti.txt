Sri Lankan Kottu Roti

Description: A popular street food made by chopping flatbread (godamba roti) together with vegetables, egg, and meat on a hot griddle, seasoned with curry spices. Famous for the rhythmic chopping sound during preparation.

Ingredients (serves 2):
- 3-4 godamba roti (or any flatbread), sliced into strips
- 200g chicken or vegetables, chopped and cooked
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1/2 carrot, julienned
- 1/2 cup cabbage, shredded
- 2 tsp curry powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil on a large flat griddle or pan. Sauté onion until soft.
2. Add carrot and cabbage, stir-fry briefly.
3. Push vegetables aside, pour in beaten eggs, scramble.
4. Add cooked chicken/vegetables, curry powder, chili powder, and soy sauce.
5. Add roti strips and mix everything together, chopping and tossing with two spatulas to combine evenly.
6. Season with salt, serve hot with a side of curry sauce.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to 1/4 tsp.
- Medium (3): 1 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): add 1.5-2 tsp chili powder and a chopped green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 520 kcal | Protein: 24g | Fat: 22g | Carbohydrates: 58g | Sodium: 620mg
