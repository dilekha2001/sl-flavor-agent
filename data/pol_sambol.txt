Sri Lankan Coconut Sambol (Pol Sambol)

Description: A spicy, raw condiment made from fresh grated coconut, chili, and lime. Served as an accompaniment to almost every Sri Lankan meal, especially with rice and hoppers.

Ingredients (serves 4 as a side):
- 1 cup fresh grated coconut
- 1-2 tsp chili powder or 2-3 fresh red chilies
- 1 small onion or 3 shallots, finely chopped
- 1 tsp lime juice
- 1 small piece of Maldive fish (dried tuna flakes), optional
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Combine grated coconut, chili powder, onion, and Maldive fish flakes in a bowl.
2. Add salt and lime juice.
3. Mix well using your hands or a mortar and pestle, mashing slightly to release flavors.
4. Adjust lime and salt to taste before serving.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): use 1/4 tsp chili powder or omit fresh chilies.
- Medium (3): 1 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): 2 tsp chili powder or 3 fresh chilies, blended in.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 120 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 11g | Carbohydrates: 5g | Sodium: 180mg
