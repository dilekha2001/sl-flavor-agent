Sri Lankan Fish Cutlets

Description: Deep-fried, breadcrumb-coated potato and fish croquettes, seasoned with curry spices. A popular short eat (snack) found at bakeries and tea parties.

Ingredients (makes about 12):
- 2 cups canned tuna or cooked fish, flaked
- 2 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1 egg, beaten (for coating)
- 1 cup breadcrumbs
- Oil for deep frying
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Sauté onion and green chilies briefly, then mix with flaked fish, mashed potato, curry powder, and chili powder. Season with salt.
2. Shape the mixture into small oval cutlets.
3. Dip each cutlet in beaten egg, then coat in breadcrumbs.
4. Deep fry until golden brown and crisp. Drain on paper towels before serving.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): omit green chilies, reduce chili powder to a pinch.
- Medium (3): as listed.
- Hot (4-5): use 2-3 green chilies plus 1 tsp chili powder.

Approximate nutrition (per cutlet, medium spice level):
Calories: 130 kcal | Protein: 7g | Fat: 6g | Carbohydrates: 12g | Sodium: 220mg
