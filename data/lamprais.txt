Sri Lankan Lamprais

Description: A Dutch Burgher-influenced dish of rice cooked in meat stock, served with a meat curry, seeni sambol, brinjal moju, and a frikkadel (meatball), all wrapped and baked in a banana leaf.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 2 cups rice
- 500 ml meat stock (from the curry below)
- 1 portion mixed meat curry (chicken/beef, similar spicing to chicken_curry.txt)
- 1 portion seeni sambol (see seeni_sambol.txt)
- 1 portion brinjal moju (see brinjal_moju.txt)
- 4 frikkadels (spiced meatballs), pan-fried
- Banana leaves for wrapping

Steps:
1. Cook rice in the reserved meat stock instead of plain water, so the rice absorbs the curry flavor.
2. Soften banana leaves briefly over a flame to make them pliable.
3. Place a portion of rice on each banana leaf, top with a spoon of meat curry, seeni sambol, brinjal moju, and a frikkadel.
4. Fold the banana leaf into a parcel and secure with a toothpick or string.
5. Bake the parcels in the oven at 180°C for 20-25 minutes so the flavors meld together.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Spice level is controlled by the meat curry and sambol components used inside; follow the adjustment notes in chicken_curry.txt and seeni_sambol.txt for each layer.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 650 kcal | Protein: 30g | Fat: 28g | Carbohydrates: 68g | Sodium: 780mg
