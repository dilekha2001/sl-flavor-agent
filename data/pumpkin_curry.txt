Sri Lankan Pumpkin Curry (Wattakka Curry)

Description: A mildly sweet, softly spiced yellow pumpkin curry cooked in coconut milk. A common comforting side dish, especially popular with string hoppers or rice.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 500g pumpkin, peeled and cubed
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, then add curry leaves and onion. Sauté until soft.
2. Add pumpkin, turmeric, and chili powder, stir to coat.
3. Add a little water, cover, and simmer for 12-15 minutes until pumpkin is tender but still holding shape.
4. Stir in coconut milk, simmer 5 more minutes until slightly thickened. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to a pinch.
- Medium (3): 1/2 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): 1.5 tsp chili powder plus a slit green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 150 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 9g | Carbohydrates: 16g | Sodium: 170mg
