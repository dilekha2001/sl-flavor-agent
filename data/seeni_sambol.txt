Sri Lankan Caramelized Onion Relish (Seeni Sambol)

Description: A sweet, tangy, and mildly spicy onion relish cooked low and slow until deeply caramelized. Common with milk rice (kiribath) and bread.

Ingredients (serves 4 as a side):
- 4 large onions, thinly sliced
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1 tbsp tamarind paste
- 1 tbsp sugar (or jaggery)
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 1 piece pandan leaf (optional)
- 2 tbsp Maldive fish flakes (optional)
- 2 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil in a pan, add curry leaves and pandan leaf.
2. Add sliced onions and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally, until deeply golden and caramelized.
3. Stir in chili powder, tamarind paste, sugar, and Maldive fish flakes.
4. Cook for another 5-10 minutes until thick and jam-like. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to 1/4 tsp.
- Medium (3): 1 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): add 1.5-2 tsp chili powder plus a chopped fresh chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 160 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 7g | Carbohydrates: 22g | Sodium: 200mg
