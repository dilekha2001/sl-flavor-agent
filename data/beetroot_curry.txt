Sri Lankan Beetroot Curry

Description: A simple, mildly sweet vegetable curry made with grated or sliced beetroot cooked in coconut milk. A common side vegetable dish in rice and curry meals.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 2 medium beetroots, peeled and sliced thin or grated
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1-2 green chilies, slit
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 150 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil, splutter mustard seeds, then add curry leaves, onion, and green chilies. Sauté until onion softens.
2. Add beetroot and turmeric, stir well.
3. Add a splash of water, cover, and cook for 10-12 minutes until beetroot softens.
4. Stir in coconut milk, simmer for 5 more minutes. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): omit green chilies.
- Medium (3): 1 green chili as listed.
- Hot (4-5): use 2 chilies plus a pinch of chili powder.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 140 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 9g | Carbohydrates: 13g | Sodium: 180mg
