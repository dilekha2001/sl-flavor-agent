Sri Lankan String Hoppers (Idiyappan)

Description: Steamed noodle-like discs made from rice flour, pressed through a mold. A light staple usually eaten with curry or coconut sambol for breakfast.

Ingredients (makes about 15):
- 2 cups rice flour
- 2 cups boiling water
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp oil

Steps:
1. Mix rice flour and salt in a bowl. Gradually add boiling water, stirring to form a soft, pliable dough.
2. Knead in oil while the dough is still warm.
3. Fill a string hopper press (or piping mold) with dough, press directly onto small woven mats or steamer trays in a circular pattern.
4. Steam for 5-8 minutes until translucent and firm.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- String hoppers are unspiced; spice level is controlled entirely by the curry or sambol served alongside.

Approximate nutrition (per piece, plain):
Calories: 45 kcal | Protein: 1g | Fat: 0.5g | Carbohydrates: 10g | Sodium: 40mg
