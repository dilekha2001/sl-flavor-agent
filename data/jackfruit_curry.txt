Sri Lankan Young Jackfruit Curry (Polos Curry)

Description: A hearty, meat-like curry made from young unripe jackfruit, popular as a vegetarian main. The texture resembles pulled meat when cooked down.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 500g young jackfruit, cleaned and cubed (fresh or canned in brine, drained)
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 tbsp roasted curry powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Boil jackfruit pieces in salted water for 15-20 minutes until soft, then drain.
2. Heat oil, sauté onion, garlic, and curry leaves until soft.
3. Add curry powder, chili powder, and turmeric, cook briefly until fragrant.
4. Add boiled jackfruit, stir to coat with spices, and lightly mash some pieces for texture.
5. Pour in coconut milk, simmer for 10-12 minutes until gravy thickens. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to 1/4 tsp.
- Medium (3): 1 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): increase to 1.5-2 tsp chili powder plus a slit green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 230 kcal | Protein: 4g | Fat: 14g | Carbohydrates: 24g | Sodium: 260mg
