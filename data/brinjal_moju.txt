Sri Lankan Eggplant Pickle (Brinjal Moju)

Description: A tangy-sweet fried eggplant relish with mustard, vinegar, and chili. Commonly served alongside lamprais or rice and curry as a condiment.

Ingredients (serves 4 as a side):
- 2 medium eggplants, sliced into rounds
- 1 onion, sliced
- 2 garlic cloves, sliced
- 1 tsp mustard seeds
- 1-2 dried red chilies
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 1 tbsp sugar
- Oil for shallow frying
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Salt eggplant slices and let sit for 10 minutes, then pat dry.
2. Shallow fry eggplant slices in oil until golden and soft. Drain and set aside.
3. In a little of the same oil, splutter mustard seeds, then sauté onion, garlic, and dried chilies until softened.
4. Add vinegar and sugar, stir to combine into a light syrupy sauce.
5. Toss the fried eggplant into the sauce, mix well, and season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): use 1 dried chili, deseeded.
- Medium (3): 2 dried chilies as listed.
- Hot (4-5): 3 dried chilies plus 1/2 tsp chili powder.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 150 kcal | Protein: 2g | Fat: 10g | Carbohydrates: 14g | Sodium: 190mg
