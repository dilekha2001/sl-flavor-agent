Sri Lankan Dhal Curry (Parippu)

Description: A creamy, everyday lentil curry made with red lentils and coconut milk. A staple side dish eaten with almost every rice and curry meal.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 1 cup red lentils (masoor dhal)
- 1/2 tsp turmeric
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, chopped
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 1-2 green chilies, slit
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp coconut oil
- 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Wash lentils and boil with turmeric and enough water until soft, about 15 minutes.
2. In a separate pan, heat coconut oil, add mustard seeds until they pop, then add onion, garlic, curry leaves, and green chilies. Sauté until onion softens.
3. Add this tempering to the cooked lentils along with chili powder.
4. Stir in coconut milk, simmer for 5-7 minutes until creamy. Add salt to taste.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): omit green chilies, reduce chili powder to a pinch.
- Medium (3): as listed.
- Hot (4-5): add an extra green chili and 1/4 tsp more chili powder.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 220 kcal | Protein: 10g | Fat: 10g | Carbohydrates: 24g | Sodium: 320mg
