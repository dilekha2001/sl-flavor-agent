Sri Lankan Potato Curry (Ala Curry)

Description: A comforting, mildly spiced potato curry cooked in a light coconut milk gravy. Very common as an everyday side dish.

Ingredients (serves 4):
- 4 medium potatoes, peeled and cubed
- 1 onion, sliced
- 1 tsp curry powder
- 1/2 tsp chili powder
- 1/4 tsp turmeric
- 1 sprig curry leaves
- 200 ml coconut milk
- 1 tbsp oil
- Salt to taste

Steps:
1. Heat oil, sauté onion and curry leaves until soft.
2. Add potatoes, curry powder, chili powder, and turmeric. Stir to coat.
3. Add a little water, cover, and simmer for 12-15 minutes until potatoes are tender.
4. Stir in coconut milk, simmer for 5 more minutes until gravy thickens slightly. Season with salt.

Spice level adjustment notes:
- Mild (1-2): reduce chili powder to a pinch.
- Medium (3): 1/2 tsp chili powder as listed.
- Hot (4-5): increase to 1.5-2 tsp chili powder plus a slit green chili.

Approximate nutrition (per serving, medium spice level):
Calories: 210 kcal | Protein: 4g | Fat: 10g | Carbohydrates: 27g | Sodium: 210mg
